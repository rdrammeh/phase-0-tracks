There are four seasons. Check out the table below to see what each of the seasons are and when they occur:

Season Occurrence Description
Spring From March 1 to May 31 Temp gets a bit warmer and flowers bloom
Summer From June 1 to August 31 Hotter months and the days are longer
Fall From Sept 1 to Nov 30 Temp cools down and deciduous trees began to shed
Winter From Dec 1 to Feb 28 Colder months and we get snow in some parts
